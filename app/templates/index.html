Questions, answered

What footage do I need?

A single fixed camera behind the court with the whole court in frame, 1080p — the standard club recording setup. If you already film your matches, you're ready.

Do my players need to install anything?

No. The match arrives as a link that opens in any phone browser. Tap a rally, watch it, done.

Do I need DataVolley?

No. The rally index lives in our web player. If your club does use DataVolley, Club Pro exports a .dvw file that loads straight into your existing workflow.

What if a rally is missed?

The system is tuned so that missing a rally is the rarest kind of error, and it learns your gym: accuracy improves automatically after the first match filmed in your venue.
